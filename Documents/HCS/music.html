Welcome to Music Authentication

This system uses your ability to recognize familiar music as an authentication method. The experiment has two phases:

  1. Setup Phase: Upload 3-5 music files that you are very familiar with
  2. Test Phase: The system will play random segments from your songs, and you need to identify them correctly

The data collected will be used to compare music authentication with traditional password authentication.
